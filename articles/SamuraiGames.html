4 less known or underrated samurai games you should play.

you have probably heard of samurais, not so ancient warriors of japan who fought with honor and are know for wielding fierce katanas. today im gonna introduce 4 video games that you can play right now to feel their power.

  1. Sekiro, shadows die twice.

    sekrio, well known for it's difficulty is one of fromsoftware games that brings joy to the player and people who watch the gameplay. the combanitaion of attacks and parries and countless abilities make for a great and replayable exprience.

  2. Wo long, the fallen dynasty

    wo long, though as not difficult or well known as sekiro is still a must try game for samurai fans. the shear amount of weapons types: swords, axes, glavies, dual-swords, hammers, etc gives it the most replayablility out of all these 4 games.

  3. Ghost of tsushima

    ghost of tsushima is one of the most beautiful games ever made. it follow the story of jin sakai trying to defend the lands of tsushima from attack of the mongols. unfortunatly, at the time of writing this it is only available on ps4 and ps5 so if you don't have one of them your out of luck.

  4. skulls of shogun

    skulls of shogun is the least known game out of these 4. it's an strategy game that takes place in the japanese after life where you are a dead commandar and have to use your soliders to defeat the enemies while enjoying the cartony graphics.
